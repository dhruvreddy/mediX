Explainable Artificial Intelligence (XAI) refers to techniques and methods used to explain the decisions made by AI and machine learning models in a human-understandable manner.

What is LIME?

LIME (Local Interpretable Model-agnostic Explanations) is a popular technique for explaining the predictions of machine learning models by approximating them locally with an interpretable model.
